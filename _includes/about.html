I am a UI/UX designer, most of the thigns I make are user oriented. This means that my design process is geared towards a specific client, and a specific purspose. Design for me is first and foremost functionality at every level: performtive functionality, code readability, ease of access, and visual simplicity. Design for me is more of an organizational matter, than anything else. I therefore view myself as a specialist in matters of organization, planning, and taxonomies. Design is effective information distribution, hence any highly organized and efficient system is always a good designed system, and a beautiful system. Any high order of organization is inherently beatiful, because it lacks unnecessary parts. My life's motto is: "Entia non sunt multiplicanda praeter necessitatem (entities must not be multiplied beyond necessity)."
